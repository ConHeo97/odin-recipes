Instant Ramen

A staple for any college/university/young adult worker

Ingredients

  • Instant ramen packet (all ingredients included
  • Boiling hot water (You need a kettle, or a stovetop depending on if it's cup ramen or a packet)

Steps

  1. Boil your water
  2. Add your boiling hot water to your cup of noodles, or your packet of noodles to your boiling hot water
  3. Wait 2-3 minutes or as directed by the package
  4. Devour ramen
