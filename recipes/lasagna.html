Lasagna

A delicious and deceptively simple Italian classic

A beautifuly rich and saucy lasagna with a stunning bechamel creating a perfect cheesy bite.

Ingredients

  • Carrots
  • Onion
  • Celery
  • Tomato paste
  • Canned tomatoes
  • Ground meat, or a mixture (e.g. 500g beef/500g pork or 300/300/300g beef/lamb/pork
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Milk
  • Flour
  • Nutmeg

Steps/h2>
  1. Create the bolognese by making a mirepoix of carrots, onions and celerey as the base.
  2. Cook the meat in the mirepoix once the mirepoix is soft
  3. Add in your tomato based sauce
  4. Separately, create your bechamel - make sure to include nutmeg
  5. Layer your sauce, bechamel and lasagna sheets - no need to pre-cook the lasagna sheets.
