Classic American Burger

Just your basic barebones American cheeseburger

Ingerdients

  • Buns (seeded or not, up to you
  • Ground beef patty, preferably high fat content
  • American cheddar cheese
  • onion (optional)
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato sauce - alternative: bbq sauce
  • An american must-ahve: PICKLES

Steps

  1. Heat up your heavy-bottom frying pan to high heat and cook your patties. Colour=flavour.
  2. Toast your buns in the meat juice while the patties rest for 1-2 minutes
  3. Assemble your burger
